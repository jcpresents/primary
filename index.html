About

MINGO is a summer tradition that started as a community effort to revisit select internalized movies that just don’t get made anymore and once a year people gather to celebrate the past & cinema classics that just won’t leave their brain.

Rules

The rules are simple, with many ways to play. Achieve a MINGO by watching a set of five movies in a line (including diagonal) within the month. Choose the MINGO that best suits you and prepare yourself as best you can!

Classic

Choose any line of movies and watch them all.

Supreme

Choose any line of movies intersecting the freespace but substitute the freespace with a movie of your choice!

Free-Form

Choose any five movies, then watch them in any order.

The Forbidden MINGO

Choose any TWO lines of movies and watch them all.
